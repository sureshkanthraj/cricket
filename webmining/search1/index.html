Savera Hotel (4 Star)
Accessibility: Chennai International Airport : 11.0 KM, Railway Station : 5.0 KM

The Park Chennai (5 Star)
Accessibility: Anna International Airport : 16.0 KM, Chennai Central Railway Station : 6.0 KM

Accessibility: Chennai International Airport : 11.0 KM, Railway Station : 13.0 KM
