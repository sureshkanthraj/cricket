Suitability

This plan is designed exclusively for women for providing funds in time of need like marriage, sickness, education etc. All female lives between 18 & 50 can secure the policy. Physically handicapped women are also eligible subject to certain conditions.

Salient Features

Periodic returns of sum assured with facility to encash at will.

If amount is retained attractive interest given.

Guaranteed addition and loyalty addition.

Life cover continues despite non-payment of premium.

Pregnancy and child birth risk along with accident benefit at no extra cost.

Flexibility in premium payment.

Special Benefits

The life assured can avail survival benefits any time on or after its due date. If opted for future, the benefits are incremented by 11%, compounded yearly.

Free Insurance Cover : If 2 years' premiums are paid, life cover is extended for 3 years from date of FUP.

The policy has an in-built accident benefit cover. This is available even during the extended cover period.

Flexibility in paying premiums : There is an option in the policy to pay premiums in advance. A 10% rebate is also given on selecting this option.

If policy is in force, pregnancy & child birth risk is also covered. v

The life assured can opt for a pension in lieu of maturity amount. The option has to be exercised before 6 months from DOM.
