Hotels In India » Chennai Hotels » Savera Hotel

Rating : 4 Star

Location : Near Marina Beach

Address :
#146, Dr. Radhakrishnan Salai
Chennai-600004 India

Accessibility :
Chennai International Airport : 11 KM
Railway Station : 5 KM

Locational Advantage : Savera Hotel is located in the heart of the city.

Description :
The Savera is beautifully located in the close proximity of Marina Beach, in Chennai. The hotel is famous for its excellent management and warm hospitality. Besides its peaceful ambience, it offers all the modern conveniences and excellent services to ensure a comfortable stay to its guests. The hotel staff takes care of the travelers in the best possible manner. In short & snappy terms, the Savera is a great place to spend a vacation or a business trip in Chennai.

Accommodation :
The hotel has 260 centrally air-conditioned rooms, categorized into Standard Room, Executive Room and Club Room. Each room is tastefully decorated and is a riot of amenities.

Restaurants :
Malgudi: A restaurant which offers tasty South-Indian food.
Bamboo Bar: A contemporary bar that serves exotic drinks.
Minar: A roof-top restaurant that serves a range of Mughlai dishes.
Baker's Basket: A cake shop which offers a variety of cakes and pastries.
The Piano: A multi-cuisine restaurant which offers lip-smacking delicacies.
