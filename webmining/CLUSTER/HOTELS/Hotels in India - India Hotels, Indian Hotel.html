Hotels in India


India, being a reservoir of rich culture, heritage, ancient civilization and glorious past, has become one of the most sought after tourist destinations on the world map. The spectacular diversity and contrast presented by the country is simply awe inspiring. The tourism wealth of the country includes pristine sandy beaches, quiescent hill stations, devotional religious places, heritage sites, monuments, ruins of ancient civilization etc. The multiethnic and multilingual social fabric, adds to its vibrancy. The delectable Indian cuisine and warm hospitable people will make you completely lose yourself in its charm. Apparently, the country is often termed as the 'Land of Mysticism'. The enigmatic land of India also offers a sight of the pearl in the necklace, Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Keeping in mind the huge influx of tourists, India offers an excellent infrastructure of luxury, deluxe and budget hotels to the travelers. Some of the prominent hotel chains in India that match the international luxury standards are the Oberoi Group, Taj Group, Welcome Group, ITC Group, Le Meridian Group and so on. These hotels are known for their world class services and treat their guest as a king. All the deluxe and luxury hotels in India are equipped with all the modern day amenities that is at par with international standards. In addition to that, there are a number of heritage hotels in India that treat their guests like Royals and provide a completely different experience. Apart from the luxury and deluxe hotels there are a number of budget hotels in India, spread across all cities and town.
